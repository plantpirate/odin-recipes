Easy French Toast

Description

This french toast is sure to be a great way to start your weekend

It's quick to make, leaving you more time to enjoy your day

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Steps

  1. Grease a large skillet with butter or non-stick spray, place on burner at medium heat
  2. Whisk together the ingredients into a shallow bowl, large enough for a bread slice
  3. Place a bread slice into the mixture, flip sides so each are evenly coated
  4. Once evenly coated, place the bread slice in the skillet, flip and remove once each side is brown to your liking
  5. Top with butter or topping of your preference and enjoy!
