Tasty Tacos

Description

These are for those nights when you don't want to go out and spend money

Ingredients

  • Taco Seasoning, measure to liking
  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1/4 onion, diced
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix taco seasoning and meat together in skillet
  2. Cook meat on medium heat until brown
  3. Gather all other ingredients together
  4. Build your taco with desired ingredients, and enjoy!
