Banging Baked Potato

Description

When you want something easy to make, choose a baked potato!

Ingredients

  • Russet Potato
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Wash potatoes in cold water
  3. Melt butter in microwave in a small bowl
  4. Mix together butter,, oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  5. Poke 8+ holes all over the potato and rub with the oil mixture
  6. Place in oven on baking sheet and bake for an hour
